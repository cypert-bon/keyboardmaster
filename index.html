How to improve my typing speed?

Nothing is mastered overnight, and in order to really improve your typing accuracy and speed, you need to practice every day. And also make sure that you don't look down and maintain good posture to improve your accuracy.

What is CPM and WPM?

It's an abbreviation that stands for Character Per Minute & Word Per Minute respectively. CPM is the character that's typed correctly and WPM is the number of total CPM divided by 5.
